# HealthAI

NutriHealth

Platform and Target Audience:
NutriHealth will be developed in Android studio using Kotlin for the Android platform, with users who are conscious about their health and want to track their calorie intake, exercise routines, and overall well-being in mind. The app aims to provide an easy app with essential features for Android users, allowing them to easily monitor their health and make informed decisions regarding their lifestyle choices.

App Idea:
NutriHealth is designed to allow users to have a brief idea of their health by providing them with an easy-to-use platform in order to track their calorie consumption, exercise routines, and other health-related data. The app will offer the following key features:

a. Personalized Account:
Users will be able to create an account and set up their personalized profiles. The app will prompt users to input their age, gender, weight, and activity level to calculate their optimal calorie limit. This is also a security feature as this app has many health related information

b. Calorie Tracker:
The app will include a user-friendly interface where users can easily record the food they consume. Users will have the option to search for common food items or manually input the calorie value of their meal. The app will calculate the calorie intake based on the quantity and type of food, allowing users to monitor and manage their dietary habits effectively.

c. Exercise Tracker:
Users will have the ability to log their exercise routines within the app. They can input the duration, intensity, and type of exercise they engage in, providing them with a comprehensive overview of their physical activities. This feature will motivate users to stay active and exercise more to keep fit.

d. Step Counter:
The app will automatically count the number of steps users take throughout the day using the device's built-in sensors. This feature will encourage users to walk more for a healthier lifestyle. The step count data will be integrated into the overall health tracking system.

e. Calories Burned Calculation:
By combining the data from the exercise tracker, the step counter, the app will calculate the total calories burned by the user. This information will provide users with insights into their energy expenditure, and with the calorie records, users can also control their calorie intake based on their health goal and calories burned, allowing more effective and personal choices for users to change their lifestyle.

f. Heartbeat Rate Monitoring:
Utilizing the device's camera, the app will enable users to measure their heartbeat rate accurately. Such a feature is useful for users to see the intensity of their exercise in real-time, and helps monitoring their heart-rate for users concerned about it. This feature allows users to have a rough view of whether they can push on for higher intensity exercise or if they need to step down and rest for a while.

g. Data Visualization:
To help users track their progress and gain valuable insights into their health, the app will provide visually appealing graphs and charts. Users will be able to view historical data related to their calorie intake, exercise routines, step count, and heartbeat rate. This visual representation will enable users to identify trends, set goals, and make informed decisions about their lifestyle choices.

Conclusion:
NutriHealth, targeted for Android users who are health-conscious, aims to provide a comprehensive platform for tracking calorie intake, exercise routines, and overall well-being. With features such as personalized profiles, calorie and exercise tracking, step counting, heartbeat rate monitoring, and data visualization, users can take charge of their health and make informed decisions about their lifestyle. The app's user-friendly interface will ensure a seamless experience, empowering individuals to lead healthier lives.
