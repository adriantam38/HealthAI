# HealthAI

Awareness of the importance of personal health and well
being has surged in recent years,
primarily triggered by the Covid 19 pandemic. Despite the passing of the most critical phase
of the pandemic, the demand for mobile applications that support physical health management
and offer flexible exercise programs remains high. This project introduces a mobile
application designed to leverage Artificial Intelligence and advanced hardware technologies
to assist users in upholding their physical health. The application facilitates the execution of
guided exercise routines that users can perform at their convenience, regardless of location.
Its pose classification capabilities allow for accurate identification of the user’s movements
with self trained kNN classifier, enabling precise tracking of activity types. Moreover, the
application utilises the built-in camera of a smartphone to capture photoplethysmography
signals for pulse monitoring. Additional functionalities include calorie and exercise trackers
that provide activity tracking for the users. The overarching goal of this artificial intelligence
healthcare app is to deliver convenience to those aspiring to preserve or enhance their health,
ultimately contributing to an elevated quality of life. Implemented on the Android platform
using the Mediapipe framework, the app is a robust tool for personal wellness management.
The application is able to conduct accurate measurements on pulse rate and estimate human
posture efficiently, while also saving all the records to the app database for future reference.
Though currently sufficient for personal health promotion, the scope of supported exercises,
operational features, pose estimation model training and compatible devices within the app
has room for expansion. Future enhancements will aim to offer a more comprehensive and
enriched user experience.
